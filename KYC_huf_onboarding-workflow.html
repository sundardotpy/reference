Let the user know that HUF account opening is a fully offline process taking approximately 15–20 working days.

Search in Finder to confirm the mobile number and email ID are not linked to any existing individual account.

Collect HUF details from user
Collect the following:
  • HUF name
  • Karta name
  • Unique mobile number
  • Unique email ID
Also inform the user that they will later need to provide: PAN, bank statement, address proof, list of members, and a physical HUF stamp (stating Karta only).
4
Associate
Raise request in #huf-kyc-coordination
Submit the HUF client Slack workflow form in the #huf-kyc-coordination channel with HUF name, Karta name, mobile number, and email ID.
Once submitted, the user will automatically receive an email with a Google Form to submit their necessary details.
5
User
Fill & submit the Google Form
User fills in the Google Form received via email with all required details and documents.
After submission, user receives the AOF (Account Opening Form) via email along with a guide on how to sign it.
6
User · Physical courier
Print, sign AOF & courier documents
User takes a printout of the AOF, signs it, and couriers it to our office with the following self-attested documents:
  • Self-attested Karta address proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, or Passport)
  • Self-attested PAN copies for Karta and all members
  • Signed Account Opening Form (AOF)
Physical documents must only be couriered after digital scans have been verified via email.

Buying bonds
Bonds viewable on the platform. User must share bond name & units at least 1 day before the trade.
  • Unit reservation subject to MIT (Minimum Investment Threshold) on platform.
  • Exact investment amount (including decimals) must be transferred to the beneficiary account.
  • UTR details must be shared with POC for trade mapping on NSE.
  • Trade settlement slips shared by EOD.
  • Trades placed Monday to Friday only.
Bonds more than 95% sold cannot be reserved at this stage.
Dashboard visibility
Investments visible on the platform after trade settlement and dashboard update.
  • A separate mobile number is mandatory — must not be already registered or KYC-verified.
Selling bonds (liquidity)
Liquidity transactions may take 4–5 working days — offline process.
  • All reports and documents shared by POC on request.
  • App-based downloads not supported at this stage.
Current limitations
  • Referral feature not available currently — will be enabled once HUF goes live.
  • Bonds more than 95% sold cannot be reserved.
  • Portfolio summaries available only through POC — app downloads not yet supported.
HUF product is under active development. Full platform features coming soon.
