Customer emails hello@wintwealth.com to request the DIS form.
We send the DIS Request Letter via email in return.
2
Customer
Fill, sign & return the form
Customer fills in the DIS Request Letter, signs it, and emails the completed form back to us.
3
Wint Wealth
Verify holdings & demat details
We check the customer's holdings and demat details in Finder to confirm they match the request.
Once verified, physical DIS slip is issued and couriered to the customer.
4
Customer · After courier received
Fill DIS slip & share scanned copy
Customer fills out the physical DIS slip and shares a scanned copy with us for final review before sending the original.
5
Wint Wealth
Review & confirm scan is correct
We review the scanned DIS slip. Once confirmed, we ask the customer to courier the original.
6
Customer
Courier original DIS + CMR copy
Customer couriers the original physical DIS slip along with a copy of their target demat's CMR (Client Master Report) to our office.
If customer needs CMR, guide them to the NSDL Speed-E app or their other broker.
Ops Team
Documents verified → Transfer done
Ops team receives and verifies all documents. Transfer completed within 1–2 working days.
Typical total timeline: 4–5 working days including courier movement.
